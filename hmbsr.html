HMB Safety Reporter Privacy Policy

Your Trusty Tool To keep Our Airport Safe!

What information do we collect?

We collect the following information As per your preferences when you use our app:

Name: You may choose to provide your name when you create a Safety Report.
Precise location: you may choose to share your precise location when you make a safety report.
Coarse location: you may choose to share your coarse location when you make a safety report.
Camera: you may choose to use your camera to take photos for safety reports.
Gallery: We may access your gallery when you use our app to upload photos for safety reports.

How do we use your information?

We use the information we collect from you to:

Replace the old system of paper reports and to allow users to report on-site.
Lower our Airport Carbon footprint by digitizing much of our reporting cycle.
All information collected are used to assist our safety team to find solutions to any safety related issue that is reported by you.
Device location information is used to lock the app to the geographic area of interest and prevent any spam or misuse only.
You can choose to share gps data location to pinpoint the location that you are reporting or manually input description for the location.
We don't collect any information about you or your device in background.

Who do we share your information with?

We don't share any information collected with any third parties, It's only used by Airport's Safety team to keep our Airport safe.

How do we protect your information?

We take steps to protect your information, including:

Using industry-standard security measures to store reports.
Requiring our employees to keep your information confidential.
Restricting access to your information to authorized employees only.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via contact developer section of the App.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by email or through our app.
